The Best Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Recipe by: Quaker

Prep Time

45 Min

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups (1 stick) plus 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3/4 cups firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon vanilla
  • 1-1/2 Cup(s) all-purpose flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 Teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 3 Cups Quaker Oats (quick or old fashioned, uncooked)
  • 1 Cup raisins

How to make Quaker's recipe

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In large bowl, beat butter and sugars until creamy
  3. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well
  4. Add combined flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt; mix well
  5. Add oats and raisins; mix well
  6. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light golden brown
  8. Cool 1 minute on cookie sheets; remove to wire rack
  9. Cool completely and store tightly covered.
